Professional modular effects suite for VST3 & AU. Chain reverb, delay, EQ, compression, and convolution in any order across two parallel channels.

What's Inside

Every tool you need — nothing you don't

🌊

Algorithmic Reverb

Two premium algorithms: Datorro Hall for lush, organic room simulation and Hybrid Plate for dense, smooth reflections.

⏱

Advanced Delay

BPM-synced delay with Normal, Ping-Pong, and Inverted modes. Per-tail lowpass and highpass feedback filtering.

🎙

Convolution Reverb

40+ built-in IRs — churches, concert halls, caves, studios, and cabinets. Load your own custom IR files for infinite spaces.

🎛

3-Band EQ

Parametric equalizer with fully independent low, mid, and high band control. Sculpt the tonal character of any effect.

📉

Optical Compressor

Smooth optical-style compression with natural dynamic response — tames transients without harshness or pumping artifacts.

⛓

Modular Effect Chain

Two parallel processing chains, up to 8 slots each. Stack, reorder, bypass, or combine any effects in any configuration you want.
