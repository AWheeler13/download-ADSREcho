Signal Architecture

Modular Effect Chains

Build your sound from the ground up

Two fully independent processing chains run in parallel, each holding up to 8 effect slots. Drop any combination of reverb, delay, EQ, compression, or convolution into any slot. Reorder in real time. Switch between parallel and serial routing on the fly.

2 Parallel Chains 8 Slots Per Chain Real-Time Reorder Parallel / Serial Per-Slot Bypass
Reverb Engines

Two Algorithms. Infinite Space.

Choose between a lush Datorro Hall reverb with LFO-modulated diffusion for organic, evolving tails — or a dense Hybrid Plate for tight, smooth reflections that sit perfectly in a mix.

4-Channel FDN Stereo Cross-Coupling 6-Tap Early Reflections LFO Modulation Psychoacoustic Damping

Datorro Hall

Cascaded allpass diffusion network with stereo cross-feed and gentle LFO modulation. Rich, expansive spaces from small rooms to vast cathedrals.

Hybrid Plate

Combines early diffusion with a feedback delay network and subtle modulation. High-shelf filtering tames harshness for naturally warm, dense tails.

Delay Engine

Precision Delay. Three Modes.

BPM-synced delay with 14 note-value divisions, from whole notes to 1/32 triplets. Per-tail lowpass and highpass filtering lets you shape the decay character independently of the dry signal.

BPM Sync 14 Note Divisions Feedback Filtering

Normal

Classic stereo delay with independent left/right timing and feedback control.

Ping-Pong

Alternating left-right bouncing for wide stereo movement and rhythmic interest.

Inverted

Phase-inverted feedback creates hollow, ethereal textures and comb-filter effects.

Convolution Engine

Real Spaces. Real Sound.

Drop your signal into any of 45 meticulously captured impulse responses. Each one is a precise acoustic fingerprint of an actual physical space.

Load your own custom IR files to place your audio in any space imaginable. A parking garage, a shipping container, the inside of a piano - if you can capture it, ADSR Echo can convolve it.

45 Built-In IRs FFT Convolution Custom IR Loading Zero-Latency Processing

Captured Environments

Grand cathedrals, concert halls, underground caves, recording studios, and guitar cabinets — each impulse response preserves the exact reflection pattern, decay curve, and tonal coloring of the original space.

Custom IR Import

Load any WAV impulse response file directly into the signal chain. Stack convolution with algorithmic reverb and delay across the dual processing chains for hybrid spaces that don't exist in the real world.

Tone Shaping

Shape & Control

Sculpt the tonal character of your effect chain with a 3-band parametric EQ, then tame dynamics with a smooth optical compressor. Place either anywhere in the signal chain.

3-Band Parametric EQ

Low shelf (20–500 Hz), mid peak (200 Hz–8 kHz), high shelf (2–20 kHz). Each band offers a full 24 dB range for surgical or dramatic shaping.

Optical Compressor

Frequency-weighted detection with a soft-knee design and dynamic time constants. Tames transients naturally without pumping or harsh artifacts.
